BackPacking Through Europe

Your Journey Backpacking Through Europe

Your journey begins in the lively city of Naples, where you’ll explore bustling markets, historic castles like Castel dell'Ovo, and indulge in authentic Neapolitan pizza. Just a short distance away, you’ll step into the past at Pompeii, walking ancient streets preserved by volcanic ash, with hauntingly intact homes, temples, and theaters. In Rome, you’ll find yourself surrounded by history—from the mighty Colosseum and the Roman Forum to the Trevi Fountain and Michelangelo’s Sistine Chapel in the Vatican.

Hop on a train to reach Pisa, where the famous Leaning Tower awaits, along with peaceful piazzas and Romanesque architecture. From there, spend the night in Venice, the floating city, where you can glide through serene canals, explore narrow alleyways, and marvel at St. Mark’s Basilica and the Rialto Bridge. Next, a breathtaking train ride through the Alps brings you to Lucerne, Switzerland, where you’ll enjoy the charm of the Old Town, cross the iconic Chapel Bridge, and take in panoramic mountain views from Mount Pilatus or Lake Lucerne.

Finally, board your last train to Paris, the City of Light, where you’ll stroll along the Seine, take in the Eiffel Tower, visit the Louvre, and savor the romance and culture of one of Europe’s most iconic capitals. Traveling by train between each destination adds comfort, scenic beauty, and a classic European charm to your unforgettable adventure.

Itenary at a Glance

Four Nights in Naples

Day 1-4

You arrive in Naples and spend your first day soaking in the city's vibrant energy—strolling through its historic streets, savoring authentic Neapolitan pizza, and visiting landmarks like Spaccanapoli and Castel Nuovo.

On day two, take a short trip to the ancient ruins of Pompeii, where you’ll walk through remarkably preserved streets frozen in time by the eruption of Mount Vesuvius

Day three brings relaxation along Naples’ scenic coastline, where you can unwind on the city’s sun-soaked beaches with views of the sparkling Tyrrhenian Sea.

On your final day, journey to Rome to explore the iconic Roman Colosseum, stepping into the heart of ancient gladiator history and the grandeur of Imperial Rome.

Two Nights in Venice

Day 5-6

On day five, travel to Pisa to see the famous Leaning Tower and explore the charming city center.

Later, take the train to Venice, where you’ll arrive just in time for an evening stroll through its magical canals and winding alleyways.

Spend the night in Venice, then enjoy more exploring on day six—visit St. Mark’s Basilica, take a gondola ride, or simply get lost in the city’s unique beauty. On day seven, it’s time to say goodbye as you leave Venice and head to Venice

Two Nights in Lucerne, Switzerland

Day 7-8

Spend two days exploring the picturesque city of Lucerne, Switzerland, where medieval charm meets stunning alpine scenery. On your first day, stroll across the iconic Chapel Bridge, visit the historic Old Town, and take in panoramic views from the Musegg Wall. Enjoy a relaxing lakeside walk or a boat ride on Lake Lucerne. On your second day, take a scenic trip up Mount Pilatus or Mount Rigi for breathtaking views of the Swiss Alps, then unwind with Swiss chocolate and a cozy dinner in one of Lucerne’s traditional restaurants.
